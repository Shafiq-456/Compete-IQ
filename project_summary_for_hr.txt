================================================================================
PROJECT SUMMARY: CompetitorIQ (Simplified Explainer)
================================================================================

What is this project?
CompetitorIQ is a tool that helps businesses keep an eye on their competitors. Instead 
of having a human check competitors' websites, prices, and social media every day, 
this app does it automatically using AI.

Think of it as a "digital spy/analyst team" that gives a company weekly reports and 
tells them exactly how to beat their rivals.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. How it works (The Core Features in Plain English)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Tailored AI Assistants (Niche Scanning):
  When a user signs up, they select their industry (like Online Shopping, Banking, 
  or Healthcare). The app automatically customizes itself. If you're in Banking, it 
  checks news and rules first. If you're in Online Shopping, it checks prices and 
  social media reviews first.

* Sales Battlecards (SWOT Analysis):
  The AI analyzes the strengths and weaknesses of each competitor and gives the 
  sales team a cheat sheet (a "battlecard") with 3 concrete tips on how to win deals 
  against them.

* Weekly Digest (The Inbox):
  Instead of reading hundreds of updates, the user gets a neat weekly summary card 
  categorized by topic (e.g., "Pricing Changes," "New Products," "Job Openings").

* Chat with your Competitor Data:
  Users can type questions to an AI assistant—like "Did competitor X raise their 
  prices last week?"—and the AI looks at the stored data to give an instant answer.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. The Technology Behind It (For the Technical Recruiter)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Frontend (What users see): 
  - Next.js 16 & React 19 (for a fast, modern web experience).
  - Tailwind CSS v4 & Framer Motion (for smooth, gorgeous visual animations and a 
    futuristic "Aurora" color theme).
  
* Backend (How it handles data):
  - Prisma & SQLite (to store competitor details safely).
  - Secure Login System (keeps user data private and separate so companies can't 
    peek at each other's research).
  - AI Integrations (uses AI SDKs to scan and extract clean info from text).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Why this matters (The Business Value)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Saves Time: Replaces hours of manual web-surfing with automated daily tracking.
* Drives Sales: Equips sales reps with instant counter-arguments when pitching to 
  clients.
* Prevents Surprises: Alerts the company immediately if a competitor drops their 
  prices or launches a new product.
